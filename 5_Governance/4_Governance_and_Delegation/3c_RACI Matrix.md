# RACI Matrix
#### `R` **Responsible:** Executes the task or decision.
#### `A` **Accountable:** Ultimately answerable for the task’s success.
#### `C` **Consulted:** Provides input or expertise.
#### `I` **Informed:** Kept updated on progress or outcomes.

- **Updated:** 20250821-1215
---

### 1. Change Request Initiation
- **Responsible:** Requesting Stakeholder/Team
- **Accountable:** Requesting Stakeholder/Team
- **Consulted:** CAE13 Contact Person
- **Informed:** CAE13 Developer/Admin
